Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Shopify SEO services

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

Typically, you'll start seeing initial improvements within 2-3 months, but significant results usually appear between 4-6 months. SEO is a long-term strategy that builds momentum over time.

Do you work with Shopify stores in any niche?

Yes, we have experience working with Shopify stores across various industries including fashion, electronics, beauty, home goods, and more. Our strategies are tailored to your specific niche and target audience.

Will I need to provide access to my Shopify store?

Yes, we'll need staff access to your Shopify admin to implement technical optimizations. You can revoke access at any time, and we follow strict security protocols to ensure your store's safety.

How do you measure SEO success?

We track key performance indicators including organic traffic, keyword rankings, conversion rates, and revenue from organic search. You'll receive detailed monthly reports showing progress toward your goals.

Do you offer custom SEO plans?

Absolutely. While our standard packages work for most stores, we can create a custom plan tailored to your specific needs, goals, and budget. Contact us to discuss custom options.
